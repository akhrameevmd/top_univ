ECON 201-0 Introduction to Macroeconomics Scarcity and
choice; elements of demand and supply, determinants of
aggregate output, employment, inflation, growth, and
international balance of payments.
ECON 311-0 Macroeconomics Macroeconomics and monetary
policy. Behavior of the economy as a whole. Income,
inflation, unemployment, and growth; consumption,
investment, and rate of interest; monetary and fiscal policy.
Prerequisites: 201, 202, MATH 220.
ECON 316-0 Advanced Topics in Macroeconomics Topics
may include growth, business cycles, unemployment and
job search, monetary economics, macroeconomic policy,
intertemporal choice, and general equilibrium. Prerequisites:
281; 310-1,2; 311; MATH 224 and 230.
