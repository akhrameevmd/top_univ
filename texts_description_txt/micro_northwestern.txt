ECON 202-0 Introduction to Microeconomics Consumers’
and producers’ influence on structure of output and prices
and distribution of income. Social efficiency in resource
allocation. Government impact on allocative efficiency and
distributive equity. Prerequisite: 201.
ECON 310-1,2 Microeconomics 1. Consumer behavior and
the theory of demand; production, cost, supply functions;
competitive equilibrium; monopoly. Prerequisites: 201,
202, MATH 220. 2. Social choice theory, applications of
elementary game theory, general equilibrium in perfectly
competitive markets, and the economic consequences
of uncertainty and imperfect information. Prerequisite:
310-1.
