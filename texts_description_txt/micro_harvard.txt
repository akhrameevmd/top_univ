ECON E-10a (Principles of Economics)
Course Objectives: The main objectives of the course are:
 Explain and analyze the role of markets, government, and other institutions in our lives
 Study the economic principles that attempt to elucidate the complex choices that households, businesses, and societies make in their pursuit of happiness and greater common good
 Critically examine the role of government policies in achieving greatest common good both at micro and macro levels
 Learn about the tools and methodologies that economists use as social scientists to arrive at economic theories.
