Ec 122. Econometrics. 9 units (3-0-6); first term. Prerequisites: Ma 3.
The application of statistical techniques to the analysis of economic data.
Instructor: Sherman. 
SS 213 abc. Financial Economics. 9 units (3-2-4); first, second, third
terms. First term: asset pricing theory, statistical tests on historical data
and evidence from financial markets experiments. Second term: financial
econometrics, with emphasis on applications to risk management. Third
term: general equilibrium foundations of asset and option pricing theory.
