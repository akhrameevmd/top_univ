ECON 19800. Introduction to Microeconomics. 100 Units.
By way of economic theory, applications, and contemporary issues, this course treats (1) the behavior
and decision making on the part of individuals, business firms, and governments; and (2) the function of
costs, prices, incentives, and markets in the American economy. We discuss contemporary topics (e.g.,
distribution of income, the environment, education, sports, health care).
Instructor(s): A. Sanderson, J. List
